FlexFWD provides users with the opportunity to record, track, inspire and be inspired by athletes of all levels and across multiple platforms. We then connect this motivated, healthoriented audience with relevant advertiser

Athlete Motivation
  • Opportunity to log, search, and save workouts.
  • Can easily find and compare benchmarks for self and other athletes.
  • Ability to comment and contribute within the community
Trainer Data
  • Coaches and trainers can easily monitor athlete progress
  • Excellent forum for continued motivation and group creation within a geographical territory or cross-functional sport.
Advertisers

  • Site sponsorship of specific areas of site according to promotion

FlexFWD cultivates motivation through relatable people and tangible, visual progress. Users enter their data and the system creates compare pallets, historical comparisons, and visible records of workouts.

Users can follow athletes who inspire and motivate them, or other users in their age and fitness bracket who have similar goals. FlexFWD leverages the power of community and the ease of the Internet to enhance individual fitness goals and capabilities.

FlexFWD creates accountability through logging, photo and video uploading and sharing.

Friendly competition is facilitated viaa sharing feature for Crossfit, Running, Weightlifting, and other sportsIntegration of GPS related devices: Integration iPhone, Android, Garmin, Nike +, etc.

Unlike other fitness sites, FlexFWD allows users totrack their workout data in a community setting.Want to compare your Crossfit Fran time to othersat your gym? No problem. Want to see what percentile you’re in for rowing? You can compare your data with a like minded community of fitness enthusiasts.

FlexFWD is the only website that tracks workoutsfor runners, triathletes, crossfitters, swimmers and cyclists. The site is rich with video, photos, and communication.

A sleek and sophisticated user interface allows users to compare their own personal bests while pursuing the personal bests in their gym, age pursuing agecategory, region…and even country. This facilitates goal setting and knowledge building in an easy,clean capacity.
